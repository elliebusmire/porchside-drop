Questions? We've Got You.

What area do you serve?

+

We serve the greater Sacramento area within 40 miles of Roseville, including Rocklin, Lincoln, Granite Bay, Loomis, Folsom, El Dorado Hills, Auburn, Cameron Park, Sacramento, Elk Grove, Rancho Cordova, Citrus Heights, Davis, Woodland, and West Sacramento.

What if my pumpkins rot or get damaged?

+

We've got you covered! If any pumpkins go bad during the season, let us know and we'll swing by to replace them at no extra charge.

Do I need to be home during delivery?

+

Nope! We handle everything outside. Just make sure we can access your porch and we'll take care of the rest. We'll send you a photo when we're done so you can see the magic.

When does removal happen?

+

For fall displays, we handle removal the week after Thanksgiving. For winter displays, removal happens the week after New Year's. It's all included in your package — no extra charge.

Can I customize my display?

+

Absolutely! You choose your color palette when you order, and there's a special instructions field where you can tell us about your porch layout, preferences, or any specific vision you have. You can also upload a photo of your porch after checkout so we can design with your space in mind.

Do you offer a neighborhood discount?

+

Yes! If 3 or more houses on the same street all book, everyone saves 15%. Just mention your neighbors' names when you order and we'll apply the discount. The more the merrier — and your whole street will look incredible.

How do I pay?

+

Payment is handled securely through Stripe when you reserve your spot. Your card is charged at the time of booking to lock in your delivery window.
